Highlight #1 Versioning Control System

A Versioning Control System keeps a very well logged trace of files. For example, if a document is bring edited by a group of people, Versioning Control System will keep track of who edited the document, when they edited, and what those edits were. This system is extremely helpful for team work in a business or school.
